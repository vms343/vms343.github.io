Choosing Credz

Making the pre-publishing process easiest for you.

As a generation, we are constantly taking pictures while traveling, spending time with friends, eating aesthetically pleasing sunday brunch, or walking our dogs.

Through votes and advice from friends and family, Choosing Credz makes choosing one photo from the thousands taken a painless and enjoyable process.

Features




collage format to easily compare photos

voting capabilities for friends and family

a timer to ensure punctual responses
